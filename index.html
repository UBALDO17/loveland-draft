📋 Resort Rules

Resort Policies

Quick Navigation
Check-In / Check-Out

Arrival & Departure

Check-In Time

Standard check-in is at 1:00 PM. Early check-in may be available for an additional fee. Please call ahead and check in at the office before proceeding to your site.

Check-Out Time

All guests must check out by 11:00 AM. Late check-out may be available for an additional fee — please notify the office by 9:00 AM if needed.

After-Hours Arrival

If you expect to arrive after office hours, please call us in advance. Your info will be placed in the after-hours check-in box at the entrance.

Reservations

Booking & Reservations

Minimum Stay

A minimum 2-night stay is required on weekends. A minimum 3-night stay is required for holiday weekends. Single-night stays are available Sunday through Thursday.

Deposit & Payment

A deposit equal to one night's stay is required at time of reservation. Remaining balance is due upon check-in. We accept all major credit cards, cash, and personal checks.

Pets

Pet Policy

We love pets! Loveland RV Resort is proudly pet-friendly.

  • Maximum of 2 pets per site.
  • Pets must be on a leash no longer than 6 feet outside your RV or tent.
  • Pet owners must clean up immediately. Pet waste stations throughout the resort.
  • Pets not permitted in pool area, clubhouse, or playground.
  • Pet fee of $5 per pet per night applies.

Dog Park

Fenced dog park open 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. All dogs must be current on vaccinations.

Vehicles

Vehicles & RVs

Vehicle Limit

Maximum of 1 RV and 1 tow vehicle per site. Additional vehicles may park in the overflow lot at no additional charge.

Speed Limit

Resort speed limit is 5 mph throughout the property at all times.

RV Size

Pull-through sites accommodate RVs up to 60 feet. Back-in sites up to 45 feet. Please specify your RV length when reserving.

Quiet Hours

Quiet Hours & Conduct

  • Quiet Hours: 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM daily.
  • Generator use is prohibited during quiet hours.
  • Music and amplified sound must be kept at a respectful volume at all times.
  • Violation of quiet hours may result in removal from the resort without refund.
Fires & BBQ

Campfires & Grilling

Campfires

Campfires are permitted in designated fire rings only. Fires must be completely extinguished before leaving your site or going to sleep. Never leave a fire unattended.

Grills & Firewood

Portable grills are permitted at your site. Firewood is available at the resort general store. Please do not bring firewood from outside the local area.

Guests

Visitors & Day Guests

Registered guests may receive visitors during daytime hours only (8:00 AM – 9:00 PM). Day visitors must register at the office upon arrival. A day-use fee of $5 per person applies. Children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult in all common areas.

Cancellations

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Standard Cancellations

Cancellations made 7 or more days prior to arrival receive a full refund of the deposit, minus a $15 processing fee.

Late Cancellations

Cancellations made 3–6 days prior receive a 50% refund. Less than 3 days prior: non-refundable.

Holiday Weekends

Holiday weekend cancellations must be made at least 14 days prior for a full refund.
